WHAT IS ANTENATAL CARE (ANC)?

Antenatal care is the care you get throughout pregnancy until you have your baby. It prepares you to have a safe pregnancy and a healthy baby

WHO NEEDS ANTENATAL CARE?

All women who are pregnant need to have this special care

MUST I GET ANTENATAL CARE?

Yes, you should! This is because it could determine whether you will carry the pregnancy alive and whether your baby will live during and after delivery. It gives you the opportunity to recognize any danger that might show up while you are pregnant or around the time you will have your baby

WHERE CAN I GET ANTENATAL CARE?

All government hospitals and registered health facilities around you but you must register with them as soon as you know you are pregnant

HOW MUCH DOES ANTENATAL CARE COST?
